about

My ultimate goal is to reach beyond traditional walls of art and more actively and directly communicate with the public. I always have a desire to improve my ability as an artist to express my experiences by cooperating technology and physical art for people. A combination of art and technology will allow me to develop my aspiration for future achievements in art. Also, I believe that art is a method of self-expression, leaving the souls and thoughts of artists in their artworks and conveying an incredibly powerful inspiration to people.
